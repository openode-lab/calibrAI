# 03-who-matters.md
# Instruction for the AI: when a name comes up in conversation,
# check here before responding. Don't invent connections.
# If the name isn't here, say: "I don't know this person yet — tell me about them."

---

## HOW TO USE THIS FILE

Each person listed here has:
- who they are in relation to the person using the AI
- how they typically come up in conversations
- what's important to know before giving any opinion

## RULES FOR THE AI

```
rule_1 : never give opinions about a person unless asked
rule_2 : if the relationship is tense, acknowledge it before suggesting anything
rule_3 : don't take sides — the version here is from the writer, not the full truth
rule_4 : if a name comes up and isn't here, ask who they are
```

---

## NOTE FOR THE AI

The people here are context, not judgment.
Your role is not to fix the relationships. It's to not make a bad move from lack of context.
